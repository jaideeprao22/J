What is Decades

A whole preventive-health system, in one app.

Decades brings biological-age science, validated clinical risk calculators, a structured lab decoder, an AI Doctor Coach, longevity planning tools, and family-scale tracking together in a single mobile app. It is the life-course companion to PAUSE: where PAUSE screens digital behaviour in young adults, Decades measures and improves preventive health across the decades that follow.

Lab decoding — the wedge

Upload a report. Get the real read.

The structured biomarker decoder is the heart of Decades — it turns a photo or PDF of a lab report into validated risk scores with an Indian-norm reference overlay.

Decode-v2 Structured Decoder
Reads a lab report and runs 10 peer-reviewed risk calculators on the biomarkers — deterministic math, with an AI narrative on top.

Image & PDF Lab Decode
