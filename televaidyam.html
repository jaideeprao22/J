Overview

Specialist care that reaches the last mile.

TeleVaidyam is a mobile-first teleconsultation platform built so that a patient in a village — or an ASHA worker beside them — can reach a verified specialist in minutes. It runs in any smartphone browser, works in English and Telugu, governs every prescription against national telemedicine guidelines, and routes the most urgent patients first.

01 · Access, language & platform

Designed for every phone, in two languages.

Installable PWA
Works on any Android or smartphone browser; add to the home screen and open like an app — no Play Store download required.
Bilingual interface
Full English and Telugu with an instant in-app toggle. Medical terms and doctor names stay in clinical English while all interface text switches.
Accessibility text sizing
An A+ font-size toggle enlarges text for older or low-vision patients.
Read-aloud
On-screen content can be read aloud to support low-literacy users.
Offline-resilient design
No caching of stale medical data — content is always fresh from the server.
Secure sign-in
Email and password authentication with separate patient, doctor, and administrator roles.

02 · Patient experience

From symptom to prescription, guided.

Booking, triage, waiting, and follow-up — built for patients and the ASHA workers who help them.

Booking a consultation

Three consultation modes
Patients choose Video call, Voice call, or Chat consultation.
Specialty selection
Book by specialty — General Medicine, Cardiology, Pediatrics, Dermatology, Orthopaedics, Mental Health, Dental, and Pulmonology.
8 specialties
Symptom-based intake
A guided symptom picker helps patients describe their complaint before connecting.
Preferred-language request
Patients can request a consultation in their preferred language.
Proxy booking
A family member or ASHA worker can book on behalf of someone else, capturing name, age, sex, and relationship.
Saved dependents
Save family members as dependents for faster repeat booking.

Safety & emergency triage

Red-flag gate
Danger-sign screening
Intake screens for danger signs (chest pain, severe breathlessness). Tapping a red flag immediately routes to an emergency screen, not a routine consult.
108 Ambulance
Emergency prompt
Emergency situations prominently display the 108 emergency ambulance number.
Tele-MANAS 14416
Mental-health helpline
For thoughts of self-harm, the app surfaces the national Tele-MANAS helpline — free, 24×7.
Priority routing
Consultations are auto-sorted as emergency, priority, or routine so the most urgent patients are seen first.
Live queue position
Patients see their queue position and a running wait timer while a doctor is found.
On-device alert
Patients can enable an alert to be notified the moment a doctor connects.

After the consultation

Digital prescription access
Patients receive a digital prescription they can view and save.
Follow-up consultations
A follow-up automatically carries forward the original complaint, previous diagnosis, and prior advice so the doctor has full context.
Consultation history
An “All consults” view lists past and ongoing consultations.
Rate the consultation
Patients give a star rating and written feedback after each consult.

03 · Medicines, prescriptions & generic access

A formulary that's been signed off.

Every prescription is governed by the Telemedicine Practice Guidelines and steered toward low-cost generics.

257-molecule verified formulary
A curated catalogue of 257 clinically reviewed medicines, sourced from the Indian Pharmacopoeia, NLEM, and BNF India.
257 molecules
Prescription auto-fill
Selecting a medicine auto-fills the reviewed adult dose, frequency, and duration, which the doctor can adjust.
Telemedicine schedule safeguards
Enforces TPG 2020 — Schedule X and TPG-prohibited drugs are blocked from tele-prescription; high-risk and obstetric molecules are locked down.
Drug interaction & safety checks
The prescription editor surfaces drug interactions and pregnancy-status warnings based on the patient's profile.
Jan Aushadhi generic guidance
Prescriptions direct patients to any Jan Aushadhi generic store or government pharmacy for low-cost generic access.
Bilingual print-ready e-prescription
A standalone prescription page shows diagnosis, medicines, advice, and follow-up — printable or saveable as PDF, in English and Telugu.
PHC / CHC pathway
Patients are guided to Primary and Community Health Centres for tests and in-person follow-up where needed.

04 · Doctor experience

Built for volunteer specialists.

Urgency-sorted queues, full clinical context, governed prescribing, and recognition for service.

Availability & incoming patients

Availability toggle
Doctors mark themselves online to join the live queue.
Incoming consultation queue
Waiting patients are auto-sequenced by clinical urgency (emergency → priority → routine), then by longest wait.
Loud arrival alarm
A new patient triggers a continuous audible alarm, phone vibration, and screen wake-lock while the app is open.
Push notifications
Doctors are notified when a patient is waiting, even with the browser tab in the background.
Accept consultation
Accepting a waiting patient takes the doctor straight into the consultation room.

Conducting the consultation

Video, voice & chat
Doctors consult by live video, voice, or text chat — as the patient requested.
Patient clinical summary
The consult screen shows age, sex, weight, pregnancy status, conditions, current medications, and allergies.
Patient timeline
Doctors can view the patient's history of previous consultations.
Previous-consultation context
For follow-ups, a clear card summarises the prior diagnosis, medicines, advice, and follow-up note.
Prescription editor
Build a prescription from the verified formulary with auto-filled dosing, interaction checks, and free-text advice.
Provisional diagnosis & advice
Doctors record a provisional diagnosis, advice, and a follow-up note.
Referral to higher facility
Issue a referral to a PHC, CHC, District Hospital, or Tertiary / Medical College.
Complete-consult safeguard
Completing with no prescription and no message prompts a confirmation, preventing accidental empty completions (advice-only consults still allowed).
Cancel consultation
Doctors can cancel a consult they can't continue; the patient is clearly informed no prescription was issued.

Onboarding & recognition

Doctor onboarding
Doctors register their qualification, registration council, registration number, specialty, and languages.
Verification status
Only verified doctors can go online and take consultations.
Honorary Service Certificate
Verified volunteer doctors receive a community-service certificate with a unique certificate ID.
Public certificate verification
Anyone can verify a doctor's certificate via a QR code and a public verification page.

05 · Health education & awareness

Care that teaches as it treats.

Health learning library
Patient-friendly health-education topics, each ending with a prompt to talk to a doctor if there's a concern.
Health videos
A curated feed of health-education videos on the patient home screen.
Telugu health news feed
A scrolling feed of Telangana-relevant health news in Telugu.
Government scheme awareness
References real public-health resources — Jan Aushadhi stores, PHC/CHC services, and the 108 service.
🛡️
06 · Administration & governance

Clinical safety, enforced.

Drug catalogue administration
An admin console to review every molecule's dosing, safety, and telemedicine classification, and to approve or flag entries.
Clinical sign-off workflow
Medicines stay in draft until the prescribing authority signs them off, with a live verified-count indicator.
Doctor verification controls
Administrators verify doctors before they can practise on the platform.
Health-content management
Administrators add and manage the health videos shown to patients.
Audit log
Key actions across the platform are recorded for accountability.
Role-based access control
Strict separation of patient, doctor, and administrator capabilities, enforced at the database level.
⚙️
07 · Technical foundation

Real-time, secure, mobile-first.

Progressive web app
Installable, mobile-first, working across smartphone browsers.
Live video & voice
A real-time communications layer; the same secure room supports video, voice (camera off), and chat.
Real-time queue & presence
The doctor queue and availability update live.
Row-level security
Role-scoped access across all data tables for patients, doctors, and administrators.
Bilingual content engine
Instant English/Telugu switching throughout the app.
Instant doctor alerting
Database-triggered push notifications reach doctors the moment a patient waits.
