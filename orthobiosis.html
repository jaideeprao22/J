or·tho·bi·o·sis / noun

“Right living,” turned into a practice.

Coined by Nobel laureate Élie Metchnikoff, orthobiosis means the disciplined pursuit of a long, healthy, dignified life through how we eat, move, sleep, think and connect. Orthobiosis.com is not about treating symptoms — it's about optimising systems. Every guide separates established guidance from emerging science, explains the mechanism in plain language, and ends with one realistic action you can take today.
